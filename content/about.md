## What We Do

Japan Profiler analyzes hundreds of real traveler and resident experiences — from Reddit threads, forums, and community discussions — to identify the problems that existing guides don't solve.

Every article is built on data, not just personal experience. We look at what people actually struggle with, verify the facts, and create guides with practical solutions.

## Why We Exist

There's no shortage of Japan travel content online. But most of it covers the same topics the same way: "Top 10 Things to Do in Tokyo," "Best Restaurants in Kyoto."

Meanwhile, real travelers are stuck searching for answers to specific, frustrating problems:

- Why won't my Mastercard charge Suica?
- How do I actually get to Suzuka Circuit for the F1?
- Is this private tour guide legitimate or a scam?

These are the questions we answer.

## Our Approach

1. **Identify real pain points** from communities where people share unfiltered experiences
2. **Research deeply** — industry structure, pricing data, competitive landscape
3. **Write actionable guides** with specific steps, not generic advice
4. **Update regularly** as situations change
